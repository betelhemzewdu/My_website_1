About me

Hello! My name is Betelhem Zewdu Wubineh, and I am a PhD student specializing in Computer Engineering, with a focus on medical image processing and cervical cancer detection using deep learning techniques. My research aims to develop innovative algorithms that enhance the accuracy and efficiency of diagnostic tools, contributing to early cancer detection.

I hold a B.Sc. in Information Technology from the University of Gondar, Ethiopia (2014) and an M.Sc. from Bahir Dar University, Ethiopia (2017). Currently, I'm pursuing my Ph.D. at Wroclaw University of Science and Technology, Poland. Passionate about healthcare technology, I aim to improve early-stage cervical cancer diagnostics.

From October 2017 to September 2022, I worked as a Lecturer and Junior Researcher at Wachemo University, Ethiopia. From October 2020 to September 2022, I also served as Head of the IT Department, leading academic and administrative initiatives, managing departmental operations, and ensuring a high standard of education and research.
